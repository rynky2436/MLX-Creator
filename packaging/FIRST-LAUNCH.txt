MLX Creator — How to open it the first time
=============================================

MLX Creator isn't signed with a paid Apple Developer certificate, so the FIRST
time you open it, macOS will say it "cannot be opened" or is from an
"unidentified developer." This is normal for indie apps. Here's the one-time fix:

STEP 1 — Install
  Drag the "MLX Creator" icon onto the Applications folder (shown in this window).

STEP 2 — Open it once (pick either method)

  • Easiest — Terminal (Applications ▸ Utilities ▸ Terminal):
      Paste this line and press Return, then open MLX Creator from Applications:

          xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine "/Applications/MLX Creator.app"

  • Or — Finder:
      Open your Applications folder, right-click "MLX Creator" and choose Open.
      If macOS still refuses, go to:
          System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security
      scroll down, click "Open Anyway", then launch MLX Creator again.

After this one-time step, MLX Creator opens normally from then on.

FIRST LAUNCH NOTE
  The first run does a quick one-time setup (~1-2 min) in a Terminal window.
  Then the app opens with a welcome screen where you choose which models to
  download — image (Flux), music (ACE-Step 1.5), video (Wan 2.2) — any, all, or
  none. A tab appears only for what you install; add or remove models anytime
  from the Models / Settings tabs.
